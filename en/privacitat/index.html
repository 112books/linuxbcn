Privacy policy

Who we are

LinuxBCN is a digital identity consultancy service operated by Juan Martínez i Serres, based at Nau Bostik, Carrer Ferran Turné 1-11, 08027 Barcelona.

Contact: hola@linuxbcn.com

What data we collect

Contact form When you write to us, we store your name, email address and message content. Only to reply to you. We don’t share it with anyone.

Web analytics We use a lightweight, privacy-respecting analytics tool. It collects no personal data, uses no tracking cookies and shares no information with third parties.

We don’t use Google Analytics or any invasive tracking tool.

What data we do NOT collect

  • We don’t install tracking cookies
  • We don’t share data with advertising platforms
  • We don’t embed social media buttons with tracking
  • We don’t sell or transfer data to third parties

Google Fonts

This website loads typefaces from Google Fonts. This involves a request to Google’s servers that may log your IP address.

If you prefer to avoid this, you can block google.com from your browser or use a privacy extension.

We are evaluating hosting fonts locally to eliminate this dependency.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct and delete your personal data at any time.

To exercise them: hola@linuxbcn.com

Changes to this policy

If we modify this policy, we will indicate it on this page with the update date.
