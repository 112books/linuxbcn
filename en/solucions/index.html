Solutions for artists and musicians

Autonomous, clear and sustainable digital presence

The usual situation

You have a website, social media, content — but no system.

Concerts not updated. Biography different on every platform. Videos on YouTube, photos on Instagram, music on Spotify, and the website connects none of it.

Any change requires touching five different places. Or depending on someone else to do it.

What we propose

A digital presence designed as a system.

A website that connects what already exists — concerts, music, videos, photographs — without duplicating content or creating new dependencies.

Solutions for cultural entities and collectives

Free tools, digital sovereignty and coherent communication

The usual situation

The collective works. The digital communication doesn’t.

The website is hard to update — it always depends on someone. Meetings are called via WhatsApp. Documents live on Google Drive without anyone having decided that. Photos are on someone’s Instagram who may no longer be in the collective.

It’s not bad will. It’s that nobody designed the system.

What we propose

Organising the collective’s digital ecosystem with criteria.

Solutions for values-driven businesses

Your own digital presence, coherent and with fewer unnecessary dependencies

The usual situation

The business works. The digital presence doesn’t quite work.

The website was made by someone years ago and nobody knows how to touch it. You’re on Instagram because “you have to be” but it generates nothing. You pay subscriptions to tools you don’t remember why you signed up for. Every update requires calling someone or waiting until they have time.

The problem isn’t the technology. It’s that nobody thought the system through from the start.
