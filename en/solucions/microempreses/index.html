Solutions for values-driven businesses

Your own digital presence, coherent and with fewer unnecessary dependencies

The usual situation

The business works. The digital presence doesn’t quite work.

The website was made by someone years ago and nobody knows how to touch it. You’re on Instagram because “you have to be” but it generates nothing. You pay subscriptions to tools you don’t remember why you signed up for. Every update requires calling someone or waiting until they have time.

The problem isn’t the technology. It’s that nobody thought the system through from the start.

What we propose

A digital presence that reflects who you are.

Coherent with the project’s values. Easy to manage without depending on third parties. Built with free and sustainable tools.

What’s included

Presence and identity

  • Custom-designed website for the business
  • Design coherent with the visual identity
  • Optimised for mobile and fast loading

Content management

  • No-code editing system
  • Structure designed for you to manage yourselves
  • Updates without depending on anyone external

Own infrastructure

  • Hosting on ethical servers
  • Professional domain and email
  • No invasive tools or unnecessary tracking

Adapted tools

  • Alternatives to Google and corporate platforms
  • Integration with the tools you already use
  • Nothing that doesn’t make sense for your specific case

Who this is for

For freelancers, local businesses and micro-enterprises that:

  • want a digital presence coherent with their values
  • need real autonomy, not dependency on providers
  • are looking for a simple system to maintain
  • prefer to pay for work done, not eternal subscriptions
