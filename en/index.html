Most digital problems aren’t technical

Tools that accumulate without order. Websites that exist but don’t communicate. External platforms controlling what should be yours.

What we do

We accompany people and organisations in building a coherent, free and sustainable digital presence.

We don’t make catalogue websites. We don’t apply templates. We don’t recommend trendy technology.

We listen, understand the situation, and design the system that makes sense for each project.

How we do it

With over 25 years of experience in technology, culture and digital communication.

We work with free software and open tools. We build lightweight, secure and easy-to-maintain systems. We put technology at the service of the project — not the other way around.
