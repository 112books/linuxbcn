112Revelats

2025 · publisher

A platform for new voices in photography

112Revelats is a project by publisher 112Books to give visibility to emerging photographers.

Two annual open calls where new authors can publish a digital photobook in a collective format. If the project grows, also in print.

Current website in WordPress, in the process of migration to Hugo now that the editorial model is well defined.
