Nau Bostik

2025 · self-managed cultural space

A cultural space that deserves a tool built for it

Nau Bostik is a self-managed sociocultural space in the Sagrera neighbourhood, Barcelona. Intense activity, diverse community, constant agenda and completely horizontal management.

The current website works, but it doesn’t accompany. It’s a showcase, not a tool.

The vision

We are developing a proposal to turn Nau Bostik’s website into a real tool for management, communication and governance of the organisation.

Not a redesign. A fundamental transformation.

A system where the website stops being a noticeboard and becomes the digital centre of the space: collective agenda, space and resource management, communication with the neighbourhood, internal transparency and self-management tools for the entities that coexist there.

Why it matters

Nau Bostik is not a conventional client.

It’s a complex project, with multiple actors, diverse needs and very defined values. Getting it right requires understanding the organisation as much as the technology.

It’s exactly the kind of project LinuxBCN exists for.

Status

Proposal in development phase. Pending funding for full implementation.
