## The usual situation

You have a website, social media, content — but no system.

Concerts not updated.
Biography different on every platform.
Videos on YouTube, photos on Instagram,
music on Spotify, and the website connects none of it.

Any change requires touching five different places.
Or depending on someone else to do it.

---

## What we propose

A digital presence designed as a system.

A website that connects what already exists — concerts,
music, videos, photographs — without duplicating content
or creating new dependencies.

You manage what you want to manage.
The rest runs by itself.

---

## What's included

**Presence and identity**
- Custom-designed website for the project
- Multilingual if the project needs it
- Design coherent with the band or artist's visual identity

**Agenda and concerts**
- Synchronisation with external calendars
- Bandsintown integration to expand reach
- Manual management when more control is needed

**Music and video**
- Integrated Spotify player
- YouTube videos without duplicating files
- Structured and easy-to-maintain discography

**Photography**
- Galleries linked to external albums
- No hosting or managing heavy files

**Contact and communication**
- Direct contact form for promoters and media
- No intermediaries or unnecessary tools

---

## A possible second phase

When the project grows, the system can grow with it:

- Downloadable press kit for media and promoters
- Technical rider for venues and sound crews
- Press section with mentions and interviews
- Newsletter to keep in touch with the audience
- Centralised promotional materials

Always with the same criteria: less dispersion, more control.
