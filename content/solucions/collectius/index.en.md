## The usual situation

The collective works. The digital communication doesn't.

The website is hard to update — it always depends on someone.
Meetings are called via WhatsApp.
Documents live on Google Drive without anyone having decided that.
Photos are on someone's Instagram who may no longer be in the collective.

It's not bad will. It's that nobody designed the system.

---

## What we propose

Organising the collective's digital ecosystem with criteria.

Tools that are genuinely yours.
Communication that doesn't depend on an external platform.
A website that reflects what you do and that you can manage yourselves.

---

## What's included

**Presence and identity**
- Clear and accessible own website
- Design coherent with the collective's identity
- Multilingual if the project needs it

**Content management**
- No-code editing system for the team
- Structure designed for multiple people editing
- Easy-to-maintain activity agenda

**Free tools and digital sovereignty**
- Alternatives to Google Workspace and corporate tools
- Hosting on own or ethical servers
- Data that stays where it should

**Internal and external communication**
- Own communication channels
- Newsletter to keep in touch with the public
- Forms and contact without intermediaries

---

## Who this is for

For collectives, associations and self-managed spaces that:

- want to stop depending on platforms they don't control
- need more than one person to be able to manage communication
- are looking for coherence between what they do and how they present themselves digitally
- share the values of free technology and digital sovereignty
