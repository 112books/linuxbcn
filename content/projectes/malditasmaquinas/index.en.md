## Technology consultancy by the hour

MalditasMaquinas is a technology consultancy service
designed for those who need occasional advice
without long contracts or commitments.

The model is simple: buy consultancy hours
and ask questions by email. The status of remaining hours
and the full consultation history always accessible
from a private area within the website.

A way to give professional responses to the regular
queries that arrive by phone and are hard to invoice.

Website built with Hugo, in final polishing phase.
