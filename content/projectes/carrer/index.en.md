## The Catalan neighbourhood movement's magazine online

Carrer is the publication of FAVB — the Federation
of Neighbourhood Associations of Barcelona —
with decades of experience defending neighbourhood rights.

The challenge was to bring a consolidated print publication
to the internet without losing its editorial essence.

---

## The solution

We built the project from scratch with two clear axes:

**Complete digital archive**
All magazine issues in PDF, accessible,
organised and searchable. Decades of neighbourhood
journalism, available online.

**Web article publishing**
Beyond the archive, the system allows publishing
independent articles when the content calls for it —
without waiting for the next print issue.

A hybrid model that respects the publication's tradition
and adapts it to the possibilities of digital.
