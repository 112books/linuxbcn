## When image and technology speak the same language

Ressona was born from the convergence of two trajectories: **Pocallum**, with years of accompanying artists in photography and visual identity, and **LinuxBCN**, building web infrastructure with free software. The result is a brand designed for musicians, actors, dancers, visual artists and emerging creators who need a solid digital presence without sacrificing coherence.

The name comes from *ressonar* (to resonate): to echo, to vibrate. A digital identity should do exactly that — amplify what the artist already is.

---

## Ten services, one vision

Ressona doesn't sell websites. It accompanies artists across everything their digital presence needs:

- **Visual identity** — logo, palette, typography, complete graphic system
- **Press kit** — short and long bio, curated photos, technical rider summary, in professional format
- **Website** — static site built with Hugo, hosted on GitHub Pages, no tracking cookies
- **SEO** — canonical, hreflang, sitemap, Open Graph, structured JSON-LD
- **Analytics** — GoatCounter without cookies, private dashboard accessible without technical knowledge
- **Social media** — image templates for Instagram and LinkedIn, online generator in the private area
- **Multilingual** — Catalan and English by default; Hugo's i18n system makes adding languages clean
- **Admin dashboard** — SHA-256 protected, no external backend, everything in the browser
- **Automation** — GitHub Actions updates analytics every hour without manual intervention
- **Accessibility** — WCAG AA: verified contrast, ARIA, skip links, valid W3C HTML5

---

## The Ressona website: built with the same tools

The `ressona.eu` site is itself a demonstration of the service's philosophy:

- **Single page**, with anchor navigation and smooth scroll
- **Zero JavaScript dependencies** on the public site — all animations are CSS-driven, the only audited exception is GoatCounter
- **Animations with character**: massive gradient typography resonating in the hero, typing effect on the tagline, audience cards with random float-in, services that reveal text on hover
- **Contact form** via fetch without page reload, with inline feedback
- **Private dashboard** with Chart.js (self-hosted), visit KPIs, sections, devices and referrers
- **Template generator** for social media: 4 formats, 5 backgrounds, 6 icons, direct PNG download

Built with **Hugo** + **GitHub Actions** + **GitHub Pages**. No own server, no database, no monthly infrastructure costs.

---

## Who Ressona is for

Active musicians who need somewhere to send promoters and programmers. Actresses and actors with a scattered portfolio across platforms who want a single reference point. Dance collectives preparing a tour who need a press kit in two hours. Visual artists who want their website to speak the same language as their work.

No technical knowledge required. The admin dashboard is as simple as a social media results page — but private, no algorithms, no data leaving the server.
